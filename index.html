About Us

Hi! We are Computer Science students from Pamantasan ng Cabuyao who love baking delicious pastries. Sweet Delights is our student business where we combine our passion for baking with our tech skills.

We make fresh pastries daily in our kitchen. Everything is made with care, from our tasty croissants to our yummy cakes. Our prices are student-friendly too!

Order from us and support student entrepreneurs. We use the latest technology to make ordering easy, while making sure every pastry is baked with love.
